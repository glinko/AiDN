Three Real Directions for the Fleet + Market Dashboard

Shared brief: multi-node operator view, two modes Fleet and Market, first priority on custom model onboarding and publishing, second priority on market visibility and external endpoint consumption. The question now is not whether these blocks exist, but which one owns the top of the interface.

Locked So Far

Multi-node, two-mode navigation, full interactivity target, hybrid operator surface.

Primary Workflow

Publish and onboard models, then immediately see how they appear as offers and endpoints.

Secondary Workflow

Browse internal and external market inventory, compare price, rating, readiness, and attach endpoints.
